# Valari Statblocks

The following statblocks are for creatures specific to Valarus.

```

Large monstrosity, Lawful neutral

AC 15 (natural armour)
Speed: 30ft
Prof. bonus: +2
STR 18 (+4)
DEX 12 (+1)
CON 12 (+1)
INT 10
WIS 8 (-1)
CHA 8 (-1)

**Attacks**

**Heavy Crossbow** 100/400ft range, 1 target. +3 to hit. 1d10 + 1 piercing.
```

```
**Dragonsbreath**
Large structure
XP: 200
HP: 15 (ship), 25 (fortification)
AC 10, DR 3

**Attacks**

**Ice Blast** 60ft cone. All targets must makea DC 14 dex saving throw and take 3d6 cold damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. On a failure, the movement speed of the target is halved until the end of its next turn.

The Dragonsbreath must be manually refilled with water before it can attack again.
```

Prof. bonus: +1
STR 12 (+1)

**Attacks**
Soldiers from Tuyevka encampment, and all officers, always have a heavy spear. Guards always have a club. Soldiers from other encampments have a 50/50 chance of having a heavy spear or club.

**Heavy Spear** 5ft range, 1 target. +2 to hit. 1d10+1 piercing.
