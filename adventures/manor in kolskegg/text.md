## Manor in Kolskegg

All non-corridor rooms have 1d4 paintings worth 2d100+100 GP per painting. A player can only carry one at a time.

3 guard specops are on duty at any given time. 1 at the front door, 1 at the back door, and 1 in the tower. the other three are asleep and will need to run to the armoury to get equipped (will not have armour)

KEEP TRACK OF TURNS!
response times:
3 turns: specops start filtering in
6 turns: 11 outer guards enter front door
12 turns: 3d4 reinforcement guards arrive from streets
24 turns: 3d4 reinforcement guards arrive from streets
8 minutes / 80 turns: 1d4 ebranan mercs arrive from streets

key to treasury is held by \[important person] who will be out during the day
key to armory is held by all soldiers. 
key to selenia is kept in the armory
