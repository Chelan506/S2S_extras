**crossing the eastern border**

A lone NE horseman rides out from one of the watchtowers and confronts the players. nobody is supposed to leave without permission from a commander. rider can be bribed. threats will lead to a coordinated response. rider won't be persuaded, commander might. towers every 500ft with 4 shortbows, a rider, and a signal fire
(these guys use NE soldier statblocks but the horseman goes 60ft)
if the signal fire is lit, reinforcements will arive every 8 turns (48 seconds) in the form of 5 soldiers from the north and 5 from the south. over longer times, more substantial reinforcements will arrive

on the other side of the border, now in sudrayal lands....

Sudryal fighters use bandit statistics but with a handaxe instead of a short sword and have 13 health (better constitution). They are also proficient in stealth and survival. The sudryal captain uses the bandit leader statblock. Kamila Nazjieza.

The players approach a hidden earthen berm. Hidden behind the berm is the sudrayal captain and 9 fighters, who can shoot 30 feet out with ¾ cover. On each side of the clearing, 5 additional fighters are hidden in the foliage. 

Upon approach, a line of 10 arrows is shot 5 feet in front of the players as a warning. If the players act aggressively, all of the sudryal will attack and fight to the death. If they are passive, the captain will come out to negotiate. 

the fighters only speak sudryal valari. nazjieza speaks sudryal valari, bimaer valari, khiiral valari, and knows stumbling ebranan. 

if the players can find a way to communicate with nazjieza, she will tell them /[information] nazjieza will give the players a clay tablet etched with the sudrayal symbol and notched along the edge. this is the captain’s blessing to travel through her lands

if the players go to dzernia seeking answers, they will (for a price) learn of a fixer who brings magical supplies to /[destination] via boat. he sails down the prostyl river in a small skiff and makes landfall on the western coast of khiiral lands. the locals there are friends with him. usually, fairies with magical strength will come to pick up the goods and deliver them back to the /[destination]. fixer is named Leshev Wachowski

they can also ask the border guards. upon interrogating a guard, roll 1d6. on a 6, that guard was there when [person of interest] crossed the border, and knows that he was planning to go far to the east coast. 

when they reach khiiral lands, they will encounter fairies who speak nixian and khiiral valari. these can direct the players to /[destination]. most of the khiiral elves are on the coast and won’t encounter the players.
